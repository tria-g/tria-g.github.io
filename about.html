Meet the human behind the pixels 👋


My name is Tria Gonzales, and my creative journey started with my fascination for the power of the creative media to communicate ideas and emotions. I started experimenting by making graphics and videos for different projects. As I continued to develop my skills, I took on more responsibility and executed numerous branding, production, and communication endeavors.

As an Ateneo de Manila University graduate, I majored in Management Information Systems with a specialization in Enterprise Systems, and a minor in Korean Studies. My studies provided me with experience in analyzing systems, understanding business processes, and managing projects. At the same time, my passion for design led me to explore the field of user experience (UX) design, and I became fascinated by its ability to merge all of my fields of interest.

I'm always seeking new challenges to expand my skills and knowledge, while maintaining an open mind for innovative ideas. With my unique perspective on the intersection of business, technology, and creativity, I now bring a holistic approach to crafting solutions and experiences.

Whether I'm crafting a product's experience, shaping a brand, or capturing a video, I always strive to bring my best to create not just visually stunning work but also deliver real, lasting value.
