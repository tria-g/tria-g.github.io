It's-a me, Tria G, the human behind this website! 👋


My fascination with the power of design to communicate ideas and emotions has been a driving force in my creative journey. It all began with me experimenting on creating graphics and video editing for various projects. As I continued to develop my skills, I took on more responsibility and executed numerous branding and communication endeavors.

My educational background as a graduate from Ateneo de Manila University, with a major in Management Information Systems, a specialization in Enterprise Systems, and a minor in Korean Studies, has given me a unique perspective on the intersection between business and technology. Through my studies, I gained experience analyzing systems, understanding business processes, and project management. Combining this with my passion for design, I was inspired to explore user experience, where I am continiously learning and challenging design limits. With my background in business technology, I bring a holistic approach to creating solutions for each project.

I'm always on the lookout for new challenges and opportunities to grow my skills and knowledge. By remaining open to new ideas and perspectives, I'm able to always create with a fresh and innovative perspective.

Whether I'm crafting a website's experience, shaping a brand, or capturing a video, I always strive to bring my best to create not just visually stunning work but also deliver real, lasting value.
