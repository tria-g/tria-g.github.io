Overview
Bear Bakes MNL is a dessert shop selling baked goods specifically custom korean cakes, diy cake kits, cupckes, and cake pops. This small business is mainly owned by my friend as the CEO baker, that I partner with.

Problem statement
The client wanted to create a brand for her small business selling the famous trending korean minimalist cakes. She wants to create a brand that sells both good looking and yummy cakes. My original goal was to help create the brand perfect to her passion, but now with the partnership and team that weâ€™ve come up together, it is now my goal as well to properly communicate what Bear Bakes really is.

Role & audience
I am the Chief Marketing Officer of the brand. I am responsible in creating the branding, promotions, and communications of Bear Bakes. I mainly handle the social media platforms for stories and posts. I sometimes curate photo or video shoot projects. Also, together with the team, I assist and work together to think of product ideas, projects, and cake designs/specials. The brand intends to serve treats for everyone, with happiness in every bite. The colorful bright image of the brand aims to bring inclusivity and joy with no exception whatsoever through a friendly and sweet tone in how we present ourselves to all.

Process
Brainstorming & research
 The client (aka my dear friend) send me a couple of brand names she had thought of. It was a simple list of names and she asked me what would be nice to have. Along with the list of names she sent, there was also one peg for a logo, which is just a bear with the brand name. She also sent me a simple notes with regards to her goals and copywriting examples.


Notes given by client as well as the Pinterest board I created


Given that she wanted to sell handcrafted and fresh trendy minimalist cakes from Korea, I wanted the illustration direction of the brand to be similar to those of Korean illustration. I researched more pegs online and saw how most korean drawings are flat yet fun and absolutely cute. It did not need any textures or extra details, it was relatively simple, which makes it more hand made, just like the baked goodies to be sold, and friendly with the low detail and roughen edges.

Brand profile
 Out of the list she sent, Bear Bakes stuck with me the most. Aside from its cute catchy name, it matched with the bear logo that she wanted to have. Also, since I knew her personally, the cute image matched her personality as well. With all those considered, that was the perfect choice for the new small business. From choosing the right mouth of the bear to the perfect color, we finally came up with the bear perfect for Bear Bakes. With this, I used a handwritten Korean typeface that matches the friendly smiling bear icon. After polishing and consulting with my friend, Bear Bakes tagline and brand goals were identified as well. We finally have Bear Bakes.

Initial & running promotions
As a new cake small business competing against many other ones, I wanted to really make use of the graphics and mascot to have a different image from them. This was why I often posted Bella Bear posters and boards in our social media page. This is of course accompanied by the cake flat lays. My direction for the photoshoot was the cake to be at the center with random fun objects and stickers around it.

The original feed was upgraded when we hit 2021. I decided that the feed should center more on the products it-selves, but at the same time, I still wanted to retain the fun graphics that we post. For the photoshoots, the cakes are not the sole center piece and the shots are now closer to see the details of the cake itself. Some minimal complementary props may be used such as a grid, a cup, books, or paper sheets. I edit the photos to make the images brighter and have a yellow hue to it, making the feed feel warm and homey. To not defeat the highlighting of the actual products in the feed, I relied on the stories for these instead.

       


Instagram feed in the first few months vis a vis start of 2021

Highlighting stories
 According to research, aside from the lower cost, stories have been fast growing and perform better than feed posts. The short videos we post on our stories are vibrant and fun using different motion graphics and a matching cute fun background music. It is also easier to interact with the customers, which is what we highlight as a brand. We can create fun stories and ads, as well as let the customers answer polls, etc. in our stories. It is also easy to affirm and remind the customers in our stories. We also post their feedback to our stories as a way of gratitude for the good words they tell us.
